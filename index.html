Outbound Verification Procedures

Before making any offer or releasing any information concerning their Regions relationship(s) you must obtain: Customer's Date of Birth (to confirm that the person is at least 21 years old), AND 2 of the following primary verification items - at least one of these items must be a Recent Transaction: • Recent Transaction - Amount of deposit to/withdrawal from Dep Acct - Amount of monthly payment to LOC or IL, or - Name of Company sending a Direct Deposit Branch or Channel of opening Dep Acct / Credit Limit on LOC / Amount Financed on IL Opening date of Dep Acct / Interest Rate on IL or CD / Opening Amount for CD Customer's responses must conform to the following: • Rounding up/down to the nearest thousand dollars is acceptable. • Rounding up/down to the nearest tenth of a percent is acceptable. • Month, day and year must be within 2 days before or after the date on the system. If you are only able to obtain only 1 of the primary verification items above, you may obtain 1 alternate verification item from below: Product Type - If customer has a Regions at Work checking account a statement of, "I don't know, my employer set this up for me" is acceptable. Email address Term of CD

Hard stop: do not make an offer or release Regions relationship information before required verification is complete.

Smart suggestions:

Disposition Guide

Tier 1 NBA: Urgent contacts which must be presented to the customer and marked as discussed to be considered complete. • Discussed - You presented the information. • Not Discussed - You did not discuss the information. Informative NBA: educational, milestone messaging, and simple guidance. • Discussed - You presented the information. • Not Discussed - You did not discuss their information. Offer NBA: Offers for retention, relationship growth, acquisition, or activation. • Yes - Fulfilled - Customer has consented to opening solution. • Yes - Referred - The solution was discussed with the customer and was referred to a Business Group partner or Licensed banker. • No - Declined - The solution was offered to the customer but they declined. • Not Contacted - Left Message. • Not Contacted - Wrong Contact Info. • Not Presented - When you contacted customer, but the solution was not discussed.

Smart suggestions:

NBA Reminder

It is the expectation that the outbound call NBA is a reason to call, but not the purpose of the call. Bankers should focus on achieving an opportunity to conduct a Greenprint conversation.
